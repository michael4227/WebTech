Provincetown & The White Mountain

I found this interesting place on map, so I just drove there. It is at the tip of the hook-shaped map of Provincetown. It would take 2 hours to drive there, but the scenery is definately worth it. The sunrise by the sea is extraordinary.

Starting from here, the pictures below and on the right are taken in the White Mountain. The drive is around 3 hours, and the fall foliage is beautiful.
